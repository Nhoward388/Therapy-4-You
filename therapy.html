What is Therapy For You?

Therapy For You (T4U) is an online therapy site that provides an affordable but equally effective method of treating mental illness and depression. We strive to provide quality help for those in need and want to help make this world a happier and better place to live.


Is online therapy really effective?

Online therapy has been around for more than twenty years and has been utilized mostly by former military soldiers. Still fairly new in industry standards online therapy studies that have been conducted by the American Psychological Association have come out with good reviews showing that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy.

Are there cases in which online therapy should not be used?

If one has extremely chronic depression and mental health issues it is recommended that he/she checks in in person at a clinic or hospital for evaluation. Online-therapy may not be enough, in that case, by itself.


Why was Therapy For You started? What was the inspiration?

Therapy for you started because of a project where we had to come up with a company that would make the world a better place. We all unanimously agreed that we wanted to help reach a world where less people are depressed and more people are happy, hence our slogan heartache to happy.
